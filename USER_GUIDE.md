# R&J Inventory Management System - User Guide

Welcome to the R&J Provisions official inventory management platform. This guide will walk you through the primary workflows of the application.

---

## 1. Getting Started
*   **Login**: Enter your official email and password. Admins have access to all modules, while Staff are restricted to POS and basic inventory views.
*   **Dashboard**: A bird's-eye view of your business, showing total sales, inventory value, and active low-stock alerts.

---

## 2. Inventory Management
*   **Adding Products**: Go to the "Products" module. Ensure you set a "Reorder Level" to receive automated alerts when stock is low.
*   **Stock In**: Use the "Stock In" module to record new shipments. This automatically updates your inventory levels.

---

## 3. Point of Sale (POS)
*   **Recording a Sale**: Select items from the product list, adjust quantities, and click "Record Sale".
*   **Receipts**: After a sale, a professional receipt is generated. You can print this immediately or view it later in the "Sales History".
*   **Voiding Sales**: Admins can void incorrect sales from the "Sales History" or "Receipt" view. A reason must be provided for the audit log.

---

## 4. Procurement (Purchase Orders)
*   **Creating a PO**: Go to "Purchase Orders" and select "Create Purchase Order". Add the supplier, expected date, and items needed.
*   **Receiving Goods**: When a shipment arrives, click "Receive" on the active PO. 
    *   **Accepted Qty**: Items that meet quality standards.
    *   **Rejected Qty**: Items that are damaged or incorrect. These will **not** be added to your shop's stock.
    *   **Notes**: Always add a note for rejected items for supplier reconciliation.

---

## 5. Reports & Analytics
*   **Sales History**: View and filter all past transactions.
*   **P&L Report**: Analyze your revenue, cost of goods sold (COGS), and gross profit margin.
*   **End of Day**: Generate a daily summary report at the close of business for record-keeping and printing.

---

## 6. Security & Best Practices
*   **Audit Logs**: All sensitive actions (deleting products, voiding sales, changing users) are logged.
*   **Data Integrity**: Only use the provided forms. The system enforces strict number-only logic to prevent errors.
*   **Logout**: Always log out when leaving your workstation.

---
